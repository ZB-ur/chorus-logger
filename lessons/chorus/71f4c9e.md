# Lesson — Ship a toy domain whose only job is to prove the plumbing works

## What happened
Added `roles.yaml` (the desired-state config) plus a `hello-chorus` domain with two trivial roles (pinger/ponger). CONTEXT.md explicitly says: "Do not give these roles real work — they are a PoC." Purpose enumerated: inbox round-trip, cron persistence, pause-flag, cleanup.

## Transferable lesson
A multi-process / multi-actor system needs a smoke domain that exercises every mechanism (messaging, scheduling, kill switch, teardown) without any real workload mixed in. Naming the four things it MUST prove keeps the toy from creeping into a real product. When the toy breaks, you know it's the mechanism; when real work breaks alongside it, you know it's the work.

## Surface area
Next time you build a runtime with N independent actors, write a hello-X domain that runs alongside real domains and lists the exact invariants it exists to verify.
